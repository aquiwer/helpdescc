    • Who are you?

    We are a company that helps you create your action plan online and for free.

    Can we trust you?

    Yes, we have many certificates and are recognized as one of the best services in the world.

    Will there be new technologies?

    Yes, we are currently developing a mobile application to make using our service even easier.

    How many people does your team consist of?

    Our team consists of two people, and we are both developers of this service.

    Are there other services from your company?

    No, we are not planning anything new yet, but in the future, it is possible.

    What technologies is your service written on?

    C#(ASP.NET), PHP, JavaScript, CSS.

    Is this service your main job?

    No, we are the developers of the GOOGLE company, and this project is just an attempt to make people's life easier.

    How did you get the idea to create this service?

    During quarantine, all stores were closed, and we had to write down our task plan, since we did not want to spend the paper that was left on this, we decided to create such a service.

    Will there be any paid services?

    Rather no than yes, I repeat - our service is free.
